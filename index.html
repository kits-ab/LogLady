What is LogLady?

LogLady is an open-source cross-platform application for managing logfiles.

How to use

Simple download and install. Open file and let LogLady read it.

You can follow the log in real time

You can filter and highlight on whatever you want in your file. Switch between plain text match or a regular expression on the fly. Just add @ in the text field to escape from plain text to regular expression.
